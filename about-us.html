About Us

eJET Systems: Small Team Crafting Beautiful Experiences.

We facilitate the development and evolution of enterprise automation across various Industries. Through enterprise level integration, deployment and testing, eJET Systems creates a reference open source platform to accelerate the transformation of enterprise and service providers across the world.

WHAT IS SONA.AI?
Professionals in any organization are engaged in multitude tasks. Employees are the source of competitive advantage for the company, that‘s why they need to shift to a new platform, SONA.AI, It is redefining the role of employees in the organization. By innovatively solving core people challenges, It will help in improving efficiency & optimizing manual processes.
SONA.AI strikes a conversation via auditory/ textual methods, understands the intent of the user, and sends a response on the basis of data collected from the organization. It is based on Artificial Intelligence & Natural language Processing (NLP).
HOW WE STARTED?
When we started SONA.AI, we imagined a reality where individuals across various domains find it easy to connect and build relationships and work together to support the success of any organization. Majority of people are dissatisfied with their organization’s progress.
SONA.AI is a communication platform that individuals use every day to build close-knit communities. We also make it easy for the individuals not to miss any last minute changes and always having the required information accessible when they need it.
OUR MISSION AND VISION
Our mission is to empower enterprises with a revolutionary online software and communication tool. Our audacious view is to change the working of the organizations by serving the people doing the work. To do this, we’re building a rare consumer company, one that people truly love.
Our vision is to create a bridge, to journey beyond the campus, bringing individuals together. People will never stop demanding more and better. So we can’t rest on our laurels.
If we succeed, every individual will get the chance to contribute their unique talents to the world. This reality is possible and we are making it happen together!
